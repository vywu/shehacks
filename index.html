About

SheHacks II is Western's 12-hour all-female hackathon. We welcome participants with any background and of any skill level to join us for a day of learning, creating and fun!

How much does it cost?
Absolutely FREE, due to our generous sponsors! We will feed you as well:)

Can I attend with no prior experience?
100% YES! Over 60% of our attendees last year were all first-time hackers!

When is the application deadline?
Please apply by March 17. Ideally, everyone will automatically be accepted, but we still want to learn about you in your application!

Are there any workshops?
5 workshops in Web Development, UI/UX Design, Data Analytics, Hardware, and YYY hosted by BrainStation.

Is this event women-only?
Yes! As we are focusing on creating a talent pipeline from minorities in tech, this event is specifically created as a place for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd years to explore the tech industry in a fun, relaxing, and non-competitive way! (If you're in 4th year, no worries - you're invited too!)
